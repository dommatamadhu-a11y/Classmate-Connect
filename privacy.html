Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

Welcome to Classmate Connect. Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by Classmate Connect and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

To provide a better networking experience, we collect the following information:

  • Basic Profile: Name, Profile Picture, and Email address (via Google Authentication).
  • Education Details: Institution Name, City, Studying Class/Course, and Passout Year.
  • User Content: Posts, images, likes, and comments shared on the platform.
  • Messages: Private chat messages between connected users are stored securely in our database.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect in various ways, including to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our platform.
  • Allow other users to find you based on shared education history (filters like city, class, and year).
  • Personalize your Home Feed based on your institution and batch.
  • Facilitate real-time communication between classmates.

3. Data Security

We use Firebase (a Google service) to host our data. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, remember that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

4. Third-Party Services

Classmate Connect uses Google Authentication for secure login. By using this app, you also agree to Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us through the app settings or our support email.
